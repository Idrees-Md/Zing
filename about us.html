About Us

At Zing, we believe food should spark joy — not just for your taste buds but for your soul. Every vegetarian pastry we make is crafted with care, tradition, and a pinch of old-fashioned warmth.

Founded with passion and baked with love, Zing grew from a small kitchen to stores worldwide, delivering wholesome vegetarian pastries that blend classic flavors with a modern twist.

Our commitment is to bring joy, excitement, and quality to millennials everywhere, all wrapped up in our signature grey brand style.
